7 Prompts

8

What parts of me did I shut down to fit in? And what would happen if I brought them back?

The parts you hid are often the most alive. What would it cost you to let them out?

0 words

9

If fear or money weren't an issue, how would I spend the next year of my life?

Don't edit this answer. Write the version that feels almost too honest.

0 words

10

What am I jealous of, and how can I turn that jealousy into direction?

Jealousy is just desire with shame attached. Strip the shame, what do you actually want?

0 words

11

What was I obsessed with as a child? And what does that version of me still want?

Your childhood obsessions weren't random. They were the first honest version of your direction.

12

What is the thread that connects my most meaningful experiences?

List the experiences first, then look for what they share. The thread is usually quiet but consistent.

13

If I could write a manifesto, what truth would I scream from the rooftops?

No diplomacy here. What do you actually believe that most people are too afraid to say?

14

What are the top 3 values that feel non-negotiable in my life? And how aligned is my life with them right now?

Name the values first. Then be honest about the gap. That gap is where the work lives.
