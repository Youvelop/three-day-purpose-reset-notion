"The world doesn't need a more polished version of you. It needs the version of you that is rooted, clear, and unapologetically living what matters."

— Elias · Youvelop

7 Prompts

1

What am I no longer willing to tolerate, and what does that reveal about what matters to me?

Take your time. There's no right answer — only an honest one.

0 words

2

If I could teach the world one lesson from my life experience, what would it be?

Think about what you know now that you wish you'd known sooner.

0 words

3

When do I feel most alive, present, or at peace? What am I doing in those moments?

Notice what the moments have in common. That thread matters.

0 words

4

What patterns have repeated in my life that might be hinting at my deeper calling?

Patterns aren't accidents. They're directions trying to get your attention.

0 words

5

What have I always instinctively known or "just seen" that others often miss?

Your natural perception is part of your gift. Don't underestimate it.

0 words

6

If I could free the world from one struggle or lie, what would it be — and why does that matter to me?

What you want to fix in the world often reflects what you've had to fix in yourself.

0 words

7

Who do I feel most called to help, and what transformation do I want to offer them?

The person you most want to help is often who you once were.

0 words
