7 Prompts

15

When have I felt most proud of myself? And what does that say about what matters to me?

Pride points to values. What were you living out in that moment that made it feel significant?

0 words

16

What do people thank me for, or come to me for, again and again?

What others seek you out for is often a clue to the gift you can't yet see in yourself.

0 words

17

What truth am I afraid to admit, but would liberate me if I said it out loud?

The things we're afraid to say are usually the things most worth saying. Write it here first.

0 words

18

What's a problem in the world that deeply angers or saddens me? And what would I do if I had the power to change it?

Anger at injustice is often purpose in disguise. What would you fix if you knew you could?

0 words

19

What part of me have I tried to "fix" or hide that might actually be my greatest gift?

The things you've spent the most energy suppressing are often the things the world needs most from you.

0 words

20

If my purpose was a character in a story, how would they speak, move, live, and lead?

Give your purpose a voice, a posture, a way of being. Sometimes the metaphor reveals more than the direct answer.

0 words

21 Final prompt

If I died one year from today, what legacy or impact would I want to leave behind? And what small step could I take toward that today?

Don't skip the second part. The legacy is the vision. The small step is the truth of where you actually are. Both matter.
